My Coaching:

What we do:

This is a personal coaching service that allows you to have affordable advice from a range of topics.

I can speak to you about any particular subjects about the gym ranging from:

    Exercise performance (form),
    Exercise selection and programming,
    Diet (Variet, Weight Gain, Weight Loss, Macros, Recipes),
    Life advice with training,
    Any other tips or advice you need.

I will try my best to give a well researched, nuanced response. This can be great if you need a quick clarification. If you become a regular to this service, I can use our context to create a more detailed, personalised experience.

My main goal of this service is to be as accessible as possible and to help as many people as possible.

To be transparent - there is plenty of information online that you can find that is applicable and can get you as far as you would need. This service is meant to fill-in gaps and provide cohesive and personalied information. I have a list of great people to listen to here:.

Who Am I?

I am a first year computer science student at university with an astute interest in weightlifting.

I have been training for nearly 7 years and a lot of information to give. Mostly built from many past mistakes!

Basing my training style from pragmaticism and science, I try to help my friends and family in all things gym. I have personally coached people and would like to do this for as many people as I can as I truly love it.
